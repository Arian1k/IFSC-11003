Thoughts on Progressive Enhancement

Essentially progressive enhancement is a perspective on website that focuses on the content that is on the given website. Instead of just being focused on the building the website for the best browsers, its focused on how good the website itself is. It is made focusing on the content to start, then building off of it and adding the presentation or design of the website, then by adding features to make the website more accessable to the users. I found an article on the SmashingMagazine website that describes Progressive Enhancement. www.smashingmagazine.com.
