Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is there Life on Mars?
      Mars at one point in time was able to sustain life, but at this moment in time it does not contain life.
  • How long does it take to get to Mars?
      It can take between 7 to 9 months to get to Mars.
  • Why is Mars Red?
      It is red due to the presence of Iron Oxide, also known as 'rust'.
  • How do rover's help us understand Mars?
      They help us learn about Mars by bringing photographs, live feed, samples, etc. Scientists can use the information to learn more about Mars and its history.
  • Why is it better to send a rover instead of a human to Mars?
      Mars is much colder and has very thin atmosphere which makes it difficult for an astronaut to survive. Instead we can send a rover that can be built to withstand harsh environments.
